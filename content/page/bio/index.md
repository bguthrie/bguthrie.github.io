I'm Brian Guthrie, a software engineer, leader and entrepreneur based in New York City. I write about the intersection of software, talent, complexity, and systems thinking. Presently I lead a team at [Justworks](https://justworks.com) that helps ambitious firms grow globally. Prior to this, I founded [Orgspace](https://orgspace.io), led engineering at [Meetup](https://meetup.com), and hacked all over the world with [Thoughtworks](https://thoughtworks.com). For a time I worked with [Aaron Swartz](https://www.rememberaaronsw.com/).

I'm a software generalist: I have strong preferences about tech and process but my job is primarily to _ship things that matter to people_. I've held roles from CTO to tech support, and I've shipped at every layer of the stack. I care about craft, but I care more about outcomes, and about building ways of working that serve both the people doing the work and the people relying on it.
