# Audience

## Reader

Hacker News readers: technically literate, well-informed, skeptical, and ahead of the ball on most topics. Do not over-explain basic concepts. Assume the reader is smart and doesn't need their hand held.

# Research Sources

## Sources

- hn.algolia.com (what has the HN audience already seen on this topic? Know what they've already said.)
- martinfowler.com/bliki (canonical treatments of software engineering ideas; check for prior art on SE topics)
- Broader web for counterarguments, academic work, strong opposing voices, and factual verification
